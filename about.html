About Stackly

A Salem-born construction company with a single promise — deliver every project with precision, transparency, and craftsmanship that lasts generations.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Precision

Stackly started in 2011 when three civil engineers in Salem decided that local construction deserved world-class standards. What began as a boutique residential firm has evolved into a full-scale builder trusted across Tamil Nadu for complex commercial and industrial work.

We don't chase volume — we chase quality. Every site manager, architect, and subcontractor on our roster is vetted for skill, safety compliance, and accountability before stepping on your property.

ISO 9001 Certified Processes Documented quality checks at every construction phase — from foundation to final handover.
Fixed Project Timelines Milestone-based scheduling with weekly progress reports so you always know where things stand.
Dedicated Project Manager One point of contact from kickoff to keys — no runaround, no miscommunication.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Stackly

Key moments in our growth from a local builder to a regional construction leader.

2011

Founded in Salem

Three civil engineers launch Stackly with a focus on premium villa and apartment construction in Tamil Nadu.

2016

Commercial Division Launched

Expanded into office towers and retail plazas — our first LEED Gold-certified building opens in Coimbatore.

2021

Industrial & Infrastructure

Added warehouses, processing plants, and logistics hubs to our portfolio — serving manufacturing clients statewide.

2026

500+ Landmarks & Counting

150+ team members, operations across 4 cities, and a 98% client retention rate — still headquartered in Salem.
